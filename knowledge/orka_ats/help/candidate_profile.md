The starter candidate profile concept includes an immutable candidate ID, candidate
name, email, recruiter, recruitment stage, start date, resume link, and created or
updated timestamps. Notes are available only when OrkaATS supplies them under an
explicit permission. The exact profile layout and field labels are not verified.
